Six Degree Architects assisted the artist Balekane Legoabe in conjunction with PLNTH in the conceptual and technical process of the design for a wall applied mural at the new Jewel City Development in Maboneng, Johannesburg. The built public art project is visible on Fox Street.

The artwork is a natural scenery of a sunrise, containing the sunrise itself, a bird, some plants and trees. It represents new beginnings - a fresh start. Each day presents itself with new opportunities. The dawn of a new beginning. An opportunity to grow or to rectify the mistakes of yesterday. It is an image that is meant to instill hope and encouragement. The artwork is also meant to be enjoyed and taken in by the public as a natural scenery amongst the busy-ness and hustle bustle of the city. A moment to pause, meditate on and enjoy nature.
