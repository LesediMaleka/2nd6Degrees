The existing house consisted of 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms with open plan living spaces. The client wanted to renovate this newly bought property for when they plan to start a family.

We were tasked with the design of an additional generous master bedroom with its very own en-suite bathroom and walk-in closet, a new inviting entrance foyer overlooking the front garden with ample natural lighting and ventilation, a connected kitchen and dining experience and a redesign of the pool area that allowed for social gatherings.

The main concept for the project was to merge the contemporary vision of the client with the existing home.
